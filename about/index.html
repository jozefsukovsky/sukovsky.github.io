About

Hey, my name is Jozef. I “do the IT” for living, for almost 22 years now. During these years, I’ve been a programmer, system administrator, system engineer, team lead, manager, top manager. Didn’t like it, started over as a Software Engineer.

I write Python, Go and TypeScript. Back then, I was a Django guy, spent years working with AWS, Azure, Terraform and Ansible.

Free-time activities

During my free time I built otvorenyparlament.info which I open sourced here. Translated to English, it means open parliament. The Slovak National Council website is at least 10 years old and ugly. I had different opinions how to present parliamentary data, so I decided to build a crawler, backend and I generate views and aggregations I consider important. For this journey, I chose PostgreSQL, Django, very flexible GraphQL API and Vue.js. Data is crawled with Scrapy and whole workflow is managed with Apache Airflow.

Some other stuff I produced as a part of learning process may be found in my GitHub account.

My most recent journeys

My journey in media field gave me an opportunity to jump into a declining market. Working as a Digital Media Director, I formed Digital Media Department and we were moving many different webs and platforms in-house, into single CMS. We also introduced first fully responsive news portal in our region, consisting of roughly 20 websites. And we introduced our paywall.

When I left, I felt like I want to feel the joy of building, which I missed so much. So started delivering Python and Django services. Been working with many different customers, spent enormous time with SearchStax. Here I build the Cloud Manager and Search Analytics. Actually, my longest journey so far.

But it was time to move on. And I now help building product which helps ophthalmologists diagnose faster and more accurately in Ikerian (formerly known as RetinAI).
